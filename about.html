My Journey…

to becoming an UX designer enthusiast. For me, a UX designer brings the technology and people together.

I began this journey with pursuing by Bachelor’s Degree (B.E.) in Computer Science from the University of Pune, India. Here I was an outstanding student, who was one of the first 5 toppers in the class exams. My team and I were chosen by Persistent Systems Limited for sponsoring our final year(B.E.) project. Based on this project, we won an intercollege B.E. Project competition by popular demand in the Digital Signal Processing Department. Along with this, my team and I published 2 papers on this project.

My Bachelor’s Degree aided me in securing a job in Persistent Systems Limited, a prestigious multinational, where I worked as a Software Developer. I was assigned to a project under the IBM Department called IBM Watson – Cyber Security. Along with training in advanced Java in Persistent, I worked there for 6 month before I moved into the next phase of my journey. Here, I had an excellent track record and was chosen for a specific work profile from the very initial phase of the project. I also attended many courses such as Soft Skills and cleared its test in the first attempt.

While working on my B.E. project and the IBM project, I was very fascinated by the design of software we used and how they could become more user-friendly. This jumpstarted my interest in Human Computer Interaction course. Therefore, I joined Indianan University Purdue University Indianapolis for this course.

Here, along with my course-work, I work as a Graduate assistant under Dr. Joseph Dephazio, as a Research Assistant as well as a Teaching Assistant.

I believe that I can think from the user’s point of view and bring to the table their voice. Bringing their requirements and the aim of any software together in a new and effective way is what I strive to achieve.

Apart from my passion to design, I am an energetic and friendly person, and love to make friends everywhere I go, be it with humans or dogs...!
